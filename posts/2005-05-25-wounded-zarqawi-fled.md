There&#8217;s been a lot of reports that Abu Musab al Zarqawi, suspected killer of Nick Berg, has been wounded in Iraq and maybe has even fled the country. Supposedly his lung was punctured by a bullet. You should just read the story at [The Jawa Report][1], because they have basically every piece of this story in one place.

> The group formerly known as Tawhid and Jihad and which has known ties to Osama bin Laden appears to be in complete meltdown mode. An Islamst message board frequently used by Abu Musab al Zarqawi&#8217;s al Qaeda in Iraq claims that the terrorist mastermind has fled the country. The post was put up by frequent al Qaeda linked poster &#8216;al-Khalidi&#8217;.

Read the [entire thing at The Jawa Report][1].
