I got a new power supply from <a href=http://www.ocsystem.com/>OCsystem</a>. You can check it out here:  
<a href=http://www.ocsystem.com/40dualfanp4r.html>http://www.ocsystem.com/40dualfanp4r.html</a>.

It looks pretty sweet. It&#8217;s 420watts and it has a neat clear fan on the side. The fan speed can also be monitored through your motherboard.

I also purchased two <a href=http://www.ocsystem.com/24iderouncab.html>silver 24&#8243; round cables</a>. Hopefully these will free up some room in my case. They should also improve airflow.

I&#8217;m very excited for these items to arrive!
