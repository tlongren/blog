So I got some new processors for my dual processor machine at home. It had 2 P3 733mhz processors, it now has 2 P3 933mhz processors. There is a noticable speed difference. Compiling things goes a little quicker than it did before.

I nearly killed my motherboard getting my ThermalTake Volcano 7 heatsinks off my old processors. I didn&#8217;t have the proper tools to get them off and ended up about stabbing a hole in my motherboard. I ended up finding a tool that would work and didn&#8217;t have any problems after that. The elusive tool I needed was a simple flathead screwdriver. I don&#8217;t think there&#8217;s one in existance in our apartment.
