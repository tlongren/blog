My [ThermalTake Volcano 7 heatsink][1] and fan came today. Before, my system temp. was around 108f, after adding the Volcano 7, my system temp. is about 86f. A huge difference. I don&#8217;t think I&#8217;ll have enough room on my motherboard for another one of these when I get another processor. So I imagine I&#8217;ll end up getting a ThermalTake Dragon Orb 3&#8230;those look sweet!
