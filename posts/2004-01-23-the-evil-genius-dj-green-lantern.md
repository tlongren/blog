My favorite hip-hop dj, DJ Green Lantern, has another mixtape out. This is the last in the &#8220;Invasion&#8221; series. The newest and final installment is called &#8220;The Invasion Part 3 (Countdown to Armageddon)&#8221;. You can pick it up here:  
[http://www.djgreenlantern.com][1]

The hottest track on that album right now is &#8220;Your Hood&#8221; featuring Young Buck and Xzibit.
