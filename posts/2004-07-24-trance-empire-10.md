I recieved DJ Pi&#8217;s Trance Empire Volume 10 yesterday. I listened to it for the first time today. It&#8217;s another 2 disc set. I love both mixes. DJ Pi never fails to impress me, never. There&#8217;s no other dj I can say that about, nada one.

There&#8217;s some &#8220;other&#8221; mixes up for download on [djpi.net][1] now. If yall are lookin for something else to listen to before TE10 is officially released, you should download those and give them a listen. I personally enjoyed the Hardstyle Empire Volume 01 mix, it features DJ Daemongel.
