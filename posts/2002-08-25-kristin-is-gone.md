Kristin, my girlfriend, has moved into her dorm at UNI. She&#8217;s living in Bender Hall. There&#8217;s no way in hell I could live in a dorm. I wouldn&#8217;t have enough room for all my pc&#8217;s. Of course I wouldn&#8217;t have to pay for electricity if I lived in a dorm.

I was setting up Kristin&#8217;s notebook and I think the ethernet adapter is broken. So we had to run to a Best Buy to get a pcmcia ethernet adapter. That worked like a charm.

It&#8217;s about a 1hr 20min drive up there from Ames, so it&#8217;s not a big deal if I go to see her on weekends. Her roommate (Katie) is nice.
