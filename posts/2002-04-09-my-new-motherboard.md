I&#8217;m getting sick and tired of my Asus P2B-DS and it&#8217;s crappy Slot1 procs. I&#8217;ve decided to buy an <A href=http://www.vp6-board.com/>Abit VP6</a> + 256mb of Crucial pc133 ram for $80. That&#8217;s pretty cheap considering that the street price for the board alone is around $160. I&#8217;m getting it from <a href=http://regatta.vrac.iastate.edu/>a good friend</a> and we trade a lot of hardware, so we&#8217;ll end up even.

I&#8217;m gonna put 2 PIII 733&#8217;s in it. Hopefully I&#8217;ll be able to get them to run around 875mhz or so.

It&#8217;ll be a nice upgrade from my 2x PII 400&#8217;s.
