HTML5Press 2.0 is [available for download][1]. Changes from 1.9 to 2.0 include:

  * 40+ Google Web Font choices
  * Built-in Lightbox effect using [Slimbox2][2]
  * Smaller post titles to accommodate long post titles
  * New styles for next/prev page links
  * Show page numbers at the top of post index/archive pages

You can find a [full list of changes][3] at GitHub. HTML5Press 2.0 can be downloaded from [the official HTML5Press page][1]. Please report any issues at the [Github issue tracker][4] or in the comments on the [HTML5Press page][5].
