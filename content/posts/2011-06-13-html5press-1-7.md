HTML5Press version 1.7 is [available for download][1].

Notable changes:

  * Support for &#8220;link&#8221; [post formats][2].
  * [Display the number of search results][3] at the top of the results page.
  * Fixed [display of author name][4] on author archive pages.
  * Added option to disable display of site tagline in the header.
  * Only load bxslider and related css if featured posts are enabled.
  * [Fixed image gallery display][5].
  * Featured images for pages.

Those are all the major changes. You can find [a detailed list][6] of all the changes [at Github][7].

If you see anything that needs fixed, please [create a new issue][8]. If you have a problem that you think is specific to your setup, you can leave a comment here or at the [HTML5Press page][1].
