I got my notebook for my graduation present yesterday. It&#8217;s a Sony Vaio GRX500. I also got a really nice carrying bag and a wireless optical mouse with it.

Processor: 1.6ghz P4-M  
RAM: 512mb  
HD: 40gb  
Optical: CD-RW/DVD-ROM  
Display: 16.1&#8243; UXGA lcd  
Video: ATI Radeon 7500 Mobility

It&#8217;s a very nice machine. The display on it is just amazing. It&#8217;s not even very heavy. It looks sweeter than anything I&#8217;ve ever seen before. If you&#8217;re in the market for a new notebook, I definately suggest you get one of these bad boys.
