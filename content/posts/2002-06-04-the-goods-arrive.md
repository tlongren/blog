I got my new PSU and round cables today. I must admit, the round cables are sweet. I didn&#8217;t think they&#8217;d be as cool as they are. They look awesome in my case. Of course the old ribbon scsi cables doesn&#8217;t help the looks of the case. I would get a round scsi cable if they didn&#8217;t cost $40.

I was kinda disappointed with the PSU. It&#8217;s not very heavy, and it only had 5 regular sized molex connectors. My last psu (an antec 300watt) had 6. I need all the molex connectors I can get. I guess I&#8217;ll have to go buy a few &#8216;y&#8217; connectors at radio shack tomorrow.
