There&#8217;s lots of ugly news going around about popular pain reliever [Vioxx][1]. Supposedly 55,000 people have died from complications developed from taking the drug.

I&#8217;ll probably never take any sort of drug like this. Drug companies are one of the most evil entities in our country. They mass produce drugs that dull senses, make pain go away, and who knows what else. I&#8217;m almost afraid the government will (if they haven&#8217;t already) make use of these drugs to better control us and our actions.

Maybe I&#8217;m just paranoid.
