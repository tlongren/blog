I&#8217;m now convinced that there&#8217;s something seriously screwed with the cell phone networks here in central Iowa. Most of the people I talk to have had this happening to them for the last couple of weeks. Anyway, my cell phone never rings when somebody calls it. It&#8217;ll just alert me that I have a new voice mail, but the phone never rings!

My phone has been wringing twice a day or so, but I always end up with more voice mails than that. It&#8217;s so annoying. I&#8217;m really curious as to whose and how many calls I&#8217;ve completely missed.
