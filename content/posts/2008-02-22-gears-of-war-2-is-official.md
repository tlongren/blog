I&#8217;m a bit late and [it&#8217;s][1] been [covered][2] a lot already, but since this is the first official teaser [video for Gears of War 2][3], I had to post it.  
  
I got so excited last night when I watched this on my Xbox 360. There&#8217;s only been one other game I&#8217;ve been this excited for, and that game was [Halo 3][4]. Gears of War is one of my favorite games of all time. The [other day][5], I had an urge to play some Gears. A buddy and I sat down played through the whole campaign on Casual in a few hours. It was lovely.

I can&#8217;t wait for Gears of War 2.
