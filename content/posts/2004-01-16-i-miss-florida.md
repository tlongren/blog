My family and I went to Florida for my Grandma&#8217;s 80th birthday. Carol, my step-mom arranged a suprise birthday party for her. She looked very, very suprised when she saw everybody from Wisconsin and Iowa there in Florida for her b-day.

We also went to Disney World. It was pretty fun too. It has a lot more rides than the last time I was there. The wether there was pretty decent except for a few days. It was typically around 70, but a few days it only got up to around 40. I had a great time though. Thanks again Dad and Carol for the vacation!!!
